Privacy Policy for Mathē

Effective Date: 4 October 2025 | Bc. Dalibor Trněný

1. Introduction

Mathē is an educational application designed to supplement lectures using interactive communication with a large language model (LLM) and provide mathematical assignments for practice. The app is used strictly for research purposes to study the usability of LLM-based applications in school settings.

This privacy policy explains how data is collected, used, and stored, and outlines users’ rights in the context of this research study.

2. Information We Collect

Mathē does not collect any personal information. The app does not collect:

  • Names, emails, or other personal identifiers
  • Account credentials
  • Payment information
  • Device information or location
  • Analytics or cookies

The only data collected are anonymized interactions with the LLM, including:

  • Messages exchanged with the LLM
  • Completion of assignments and exercises

This data is voluntarily provided by users and cannot be linked to any individual.

3. How We Use Your Data

All collected data is used exclusively for research purposes to improve the understanding of LLM-based educational tools. The data cannot identify individual users and is not shared with any third parties.

4. User Rights

While users cannot modify their data, they have the right to:

  • Opt out of the research at any time
  • Request deletion of their data

Users who choose to opt out or request deletion will have their data removed from the research database.

5. Third-Party Services

Mathē does not integrate with any third-party services. All data remains within the research database controlled by the researcher.

6. Ethical Approval

This research has been reviewed and approved by the Masaryk University Research Ethics Committee. All procedures follow ethical guidelines to protect participants’ privacy and ensure the responsible handling of data.

7. Contact Information

For questions regarding privacy or data handling, please contact: dalibor.trneny00@gmail.com
